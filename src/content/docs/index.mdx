## What this is

Two kinds of writing on the shifts happening in AI and software. **Notes** are the
stream: dated posts, one per source, capturing what something said and why it
matters. **Topics** are the garden: evergreen pages that keep evolving as new
notes add to them. Notes feed topics; topics outlast any single note.
